About Me

I'm a journalism graduate student at the University of Maryland, where I'm using my coding chops and design background to tell compelling stories. I specialize in graphics, data journalism and interactive design and development, covering topics like the presidential pardons and film adaptations of books. Previously, I studied visual communication design and psychology at the University of Notre Dame, where I graduated magna cum laude, and worked in design and communications roles at newspapers, nonprofits, schools, design firms and businesses. I'm passionate about projects that serve the public good, and I love a good quiche.
